    Soil Water And Air Testing Laboratory

    Who are We?

    Soil Water and Air Testing Laboratory Pvt. Ltd. (SWAT Lab) is a lab dedicated to high quality analytical testing, technical excellence and customer satisfaction with timely service. We are a team of highly qualified water, soil and air quality engineers and scientists that have been working as consultants for various private and government agencies. SWAT can assist you to test water for your private well at your households, public water supply systems, bottled water products and soil for agriculture and land use plans.

    Why Test with SWAT?

    SWAT lab is unique as it delivers Water and Wastewater, Solid waste, Faecal Sludge, Soil, Air and Noise quality monitoring service that can be affordable and easily accessible by the interested clients.

    Features

    The SWAT is well-equipped to perform analytical tests of physical, chemical and biological parameters associated with water, wastewater, and soil samples. With quality of the test being our priority, the test results will be provided in a timely manner. SWAT is staffed and managed by a team of Environmental engineers, chemists and scientists. Our team of experts are professional and dedicated in giving high quality results.
